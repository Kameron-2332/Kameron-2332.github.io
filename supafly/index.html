SKYDIVING COACHING

One on one coaching is one of the best learning environments for your skydiving progression.  If you are a novice, create a strong foundation of knowledge to apply to your skydiving through coaching.   If you already have experience and would like to fine-tune your current flying abilities, challenge yourself through coaching.  Supafly Skydiving specializes in Freefly coaching. Basic RW and bodyflight coaching is also available. The goal is to help skydivers excel past their current flying capabilities.  Participants will have professional one on one coaching that is personalized to their learning style. Every coached skydive includes video for post jump analysis.

Before a new lesson, a detailed briefing will review the objectives and body positions for the upcoming skydive. During each briefing and ground preparation, you will visualize and physically practice maneuvers with a series of drills for better body awareness in the air. The coach flies with you during your skydive, assisting in the corrections of body positions and maneuvers. As you practice the maneuvers, the coach will provide you in-air feedback.  After each jump, you and the instructor will review the video with a complete debrief and make suggestions for improvements.  The coach will review flight techniques with you from which you will benefit.  You will be assisted in creating drills for yourself to ensure constant progression.

Head Down Flying

If you want to learn head down flying, Supafly Skydiving can get your started. In this progression package, you will experience the first steps of head down flying. You will start by learning how to properly set up for a head down exit. A pre jump briefing will get you familiar with the four quadrants of the exit set up. Creating a knowledge of visual references and body position will prepare you for flying on your head. Video is used on every jump for post jump analysis. *ALL PRICING DOES NOT INCLUDE JUMP TICKETS – COACHING FEES ONLYVIDEO INCLUDED ON EVERY JUMP HEAD DOWN INTRO 1 & 2 *$700 plus jump tickets -20 Jump Package with Coaching-

JUMPS 1-10, PART 1 – EXITS AND TRANSITIONS.

A proper set up will make exits easier. Experience good set up and exit positions by practicing on the ground. You will see what is expected from your hips, legs/feet, and head. In the beginning, exit the airplane while gripped with your coach. This will give you a taste of what you are in for. In-air instruction will provide a way to stay focused on your objectives. Through training, learn how to adjust while flying down the hill. A series of transitions from head up to head down will teach you how to “reset” if you are not getting the desired effect. Transition practice will be the best way to start head down flying from your upright position. From your solid upright position, learn the techniques used to transition to head down and see where it puts you!!

JUMPS 11-20, PART 2 – HEAD DOWN BASICS.

After you feel good about your transitions from upright to head down, you can start to fly longer periods of head down. It should be time for you to learn unlinked head down exits. When all is going well and the time is right, we will remain head down as much of the skydive as possible. Visual references and the correct body positions will be reviewed. Your coach will guide you with in-air instruction, assisting you in the correct body positions to achieve your goals. During part 2 of the head down basics, you will be reviewing levels, proximity, and movements.

Head Up Flying

During Freefly Intro 1, you will be briefed on making your first freefly jump. This program covers gear review, exit order, canopy skills, body position, and other special consideration that factor into a freefly jump. After a brief review of your in-air basic bodyflight techniques, you will be instructed on how to transition to backfly. Backflying is an easy first step to more advanced vertical flight positions. Upright, commonly referred to as head-up or sit-fly, will be the best area to begin your freefly progression. Freefly Intro 1 & 2 will provide you with this experience. *ALL PRICING DOES NOT INCLUDE JUMP TICKETS – COACHING FEES ONLY VIDEO INCLUDED ON EVERY JUMPFREEFLY INTRO 1 & 2 *$350 plus jump tickets -10 Jump Program with Coaching-

JUMPS 1-5, BELLY TO BACK – PART 1

Choose your belly exit, diving or floating, and make a series of transitions from basic belly fly to backflying. You will learn control in both orientations and then apply heading maintenance. This can take a few jumps, providing an opportunity for quality practice time.

Jumps 6-10, BACKFLYING TO UPRIGHT – PART 2

Once you feel control in the back-fly position, you can learn to transition to a more upright position. Heading maintenance will be another area of focus while going for the ultimate goal, sit-flying! Embracing each step on the way will create strong fundamental skills. Repetition is a key element to an increased learning curve.

FREEFLY INTRO 3 *$350 plus jump tickets -10 Jump Program

When you are ready for Freefly Intro 3, you will be instructed on the upright flying position. Head up flying is the most commonly used position to learn vertical flight. Start your training by learning backfly/sit-fly exits. After stability is learned while flying upright, heading maintenance and proximity control is the area of focus in Freefly Intro 3. You are not limited to only ten jumps with coaching. This program, like others, can be experienced more than once if desired. For the best results in the next step of your freefly progression, you should be consistent with the objectives in Freefly Intro 3.

Upright Basics

With the upright basics package, you will have ten coached skydives to practice your mobility while flying upright. Combining the techniques reviewed in the Freefly Intro Packages, we will add level/speed control practice. Becoming competent in your upright mobility will allow you to move to your slot, fly the slot, and finally take the grip. Upright basics will improve your overall sit-flying abilities. $350 plus jump tickets -10 Jump Program with Coaching-

JUMPS 1-5, FALL RATE(SPEED) AND SLOT FLYING.

Learn how to adjust your speed while flying upright. Levels are an important factor, especially when flying in a gripped formation. Before you practice grip taking, your levels and proximity skills should be solidified. Grip taking is easy, slot flying is the real practice. Positive forward pressure and a quiet flying position will be experienced before grip taking drills.

JUMPS 6-10, GRIP TAKING.

Once you have learned mobility and have had a chance to solidify those skills, grip taking will be easier. On grips, you will see how important it is to remain in a strong position. Upright flying requires foot and leg pressure. We will experience the techniques that allow easy grip taking.

TUNNEL COACHING

The wind tunnel has become a popular and cost effective method for coaching. It is also heavily influencing the way we fly in the sky. Skydivers often take advantage of the positive learning environment wind tunnel coaching provides. It is a great facility to learn proper flying techniques and body positions. With your coach, the wind tunnel experience can be progressive and fun at the same time. The potential to increase your body’s awareness is high, while spending up to two minutes or more at a time in the air flow. Arrive at your session early to meet your coach and review your goals to create a plan. It’s recommended to fly in fifteen to thirty minute intervals.

Communication is key, so an important part of the briefing will be understanding how you and your coach will interact while inside the flight chamber. Breaks are provided by other flyers and you will have time to discuss your progression before your next rotation. Each flight session is followed up by a short video debrief. Take a break and go fly again! You will be amazed at how much can be learned. It doesn’t end there, how will you translate this to the sky? Supafly Skydiving promotes the use of the wind tunnel to enhance our skydiving techniques. The wind tunnel compliments your skydiving progression, especially with a skydiving/tunnel coach.
