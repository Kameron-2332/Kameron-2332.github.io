Milestones

Here are the suggested milestones that will be gone through once development starts.

Part 1: Kickoff Meeting
Part 2: Phase 1 of development
Part 3: 1st Design evaluation
Part 4: Phase 2 of development
Part 5: 2nd Design evaluation
Part 6: Phase 3 of development
Part 7: Website Launch / handoff

Part 1: we will go over all your needs and goals of your website. As mentioned above, I want to make your site as specifically tailored to you as possible. Part 2: I will build the first full version of your website. Part 3: I will then make a live link for you to preview the first version of your site so that you can list all revisions you'd like to see. Part 4: I will execute all revisions you listed in the the previous part.

Parts 5 & 6 will then take place just as in parts 3 & 4. By Part 7 you will have a completely finished website that is ready for deployment. Once Part 7 is complete and your site is up, you will receive your website training. You will learn to manage your website's console, edit text & photos on the body of your website, manage hosting and manage your domain name.
